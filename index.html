SEQUENCE

Start

OBJECT OF THE GAME:

Connect a series of five of the same colour chip either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally on the board.

NOTE: The corners of the board are wild and players can use it towards their sequence.

For 2 teams: Score 2 sequences before your opponents.
For 3 teams: Score 1 sequence before your opponents.

RULES:

Set-up

The board contains 2 copies of each playing card. When you select a card, you may select a place for your token as long as it isn't already taken. Jacks are special cards and do not appear on the board.

The Jacks

There are 8 Jacks in the card deck. The 4 Jacks with TWO EYES are wild, meaning place a token at any open location. The 4 jacks with ONE EYE are remove, meaning to remove any token from the board as long as it's not part of a completed sequence

Dead Card

If you hold a card in your hand with no available space, you may replace the card for a new one and proceed with your turn as normal.

CSULB Senior Project by Thomas, Michael, Wilburn
